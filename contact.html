Frequently Asked Questions

What types of industrial fans do you review?

We review all types of industrial fans including HVLS (High-Volume, Low-Speed) fans, exhaust fans, ceiling fans, and portable industrial fans. Our focus is primarily on large-scale fans used in commercial and industrial settings.

Do you offer installation services?

We don't directly offer installation services, but we can connect you with certified installers in your area. Contact us with your location and requirements, and we'll help you find the right installation partner.

How accurate is your airflow simulator?

Our airflow simulator provides a realistic approximation of how different fans will perform in your space. While it's based on manufacturer specifications and fluid dynamics principles, we recommend consulting with an HVAC professional for precise calculations for your specific facility.

Do you ship internationally?

We don't sell products directly. As an affiliate site, we connect you with manufacturers and retailers who handle shipping. Most of our affiliate partners do ship internationally, but shipping policies vary by retailer.
