Industrial Fan Insider Logo Design

Logo Concept

The Industrial Fan Insider logo is designed to represent the company's dual focus on industrial fan expertise and innovative airflow simulation technology. The logo features:

  • Five-blade fan design: Representing the industrial HVLS fans that are the core focus of the business
  • Flowing air visualization: The curved, dashed lines symbolize the airflow patterns and the simulation technology that sets the business apart
  • Circular enclosure: Represents completeness and the comprehensive nature of the information provided
  • Bold typography: Conveys authority and expertise in the industrial fan industry

Color Palette

The color scheme was chosen to convey professionalism, technology, and environmental benefits:

#3498db
#2ecc71
#2c3e50
#ecf0f1
  • Blue (#3498db): Represents technology, reliability, and cooling - core benefits of industrial fans
  • Green (#2ecc71): Symbolizes energy efficiency and environmental benefits of proper air circulation
  • Dark Blue (#2c3e50): Conveys professionalism, authority, and industrial strength
  • Light Gray (#ecf0f1): Provides contrast and represents clarity of information

Usage Guidelines

The logo should be used consistently across all platforms to build brand recognition:

  • Website header and favicon
  • Social media profiles
  • Marketing materials
  • Airflow simulator branding
  • Email signatures and communications

The logo works well in both digital and print formats and can be scaled to different sizes while maintaining legibility.
